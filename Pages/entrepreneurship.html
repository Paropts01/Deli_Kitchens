Certificate in Culinary Entrepreneurship

Discover the art of cooking and unlock your professional chef potential.

About the Program

A Culinary Entrepreneurship Program is a specialized educational program that integrates formal culinary training with core business management principles. It is designed for aspiring food professionals who aim to launch, own, and operate their own food-related businesses, such as restaurants, catering companies, food trucks, or specialty food product lines. The program provides a comprehensive foundation in both kitchen expertise and the strategic business acumen needed to succeed in the highly competitive food industry.


What You’ll Learn

Business Plan Development
Financial Management
Menu Engineering and Product Development
Marketing and Branding
Operations and Compliance

Duration & Requirements

Duration: 3 years (full-time)
Entry Requirement: Grade 12 certificate or equivalent
Mode of Study: On-campus with practical sessions in our training kitchens


Career Opportunities

Restaurant/Cafe Owner
Food Truck Operator
Catering Company Owner
Specialty Food Producer
Culinary Consultant
