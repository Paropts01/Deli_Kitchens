Discover the art of cooking and unlock your professional chef potential.

About the Program

Professionally learning Culinary Management provides the skills to oversee all aspects of food service operations, ensuring profitability, efficiency, and exceptional guest experiences in a dynamic and globally in-demand industry.


What You’ll Learn

Comprehensive Skill Set
Enhanced Career Mobility
Leadership Development
Practical Experience
Adaptability and Innovation

Requirements & Mode of Study

Entry Requirement: Grade 12 certificate or equivalent
Mode of Study: On-campus with practical sessions in our training kitchens
