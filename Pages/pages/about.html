About Deli Kitchen

Discover our passion for culinary excellence and our commitment to nurturing the next generation of world-class chefs.

Our Story

Over 22 Years of Culinary Excellence

Founded in 2003 by renowned chef Viscem Blader, Deli Kitchen has been at the forefront of culinary education for over two decades. Our mission has always been to blend traditional techniques with innovative approaches to cooking.

We believe that great chefs are not born, but made through dedication, practice, and the right guidance. Our experienced instructors bring decades of professional experience from Michelin-starred restaurants, boutique bakeries, and international hotel chains.

Our state-of-the-art facilities include professional-grade kitchens, a wine tasting room, a chocolate laboratory, and a farm-to-table restaurant where students gain real-world experience.

Our Mission & Values

Education Excellence

We provide comprehensive culinary education that combines theory with extensive hands-on training in professional kitchen environments.

Sustainability

We emphasize sustainable practices, from sourcing local ingredients to minimizing food waste in all our culinary operations.

Community

We foster a supportive community where students collaborate, share ideas, and grow together as culinary professionals.

Innovation

We encourage creativity and innovation, pushing the boundaries of traditional cuisine while respecting culinary heritage.
