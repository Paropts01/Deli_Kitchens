Discover the art of cooking and unlock your professional chef potential.

About the Program

A Nutrition & Wellness Program is an educational initiative designed to empower participants with the knowledge and practical skills necessary to plan, shop for, and prepare nutritious, cost-effective, and appealing meals for their households. These programs focus on practical, real-world application, aiming to foster sustainable healthy eating habits and family wellness rather than solely professional culinary techniques.


What You’ll Learn

Basic Nutrition and Dietary Guidelines
Meal Planning and Budgeting
Fundamental Cooking Skills
Food Safety and Hygiene
Family Engagement

Requirements & Mode of Study

Entry Requirement: Grade 12 certificate or equivalent
Mode of Study: On-campus with practical sessions in our training kitchens


Career Opportunities

Nutrition Educator/Health Coach
Personal Chef / Meal Planner
Food Blogger/Writer
Caterer
Wellness Entrepreneur
